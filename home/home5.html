RELATIONSHIP WITH THE GHANA CATHOLIC YOUTH COUNCIL (GHANCYC)

The National Movement for the past forty years, of the existence of the Ghana National Catholic Youth Council has collaborated through the various platforms. Meetings Four routine annual major meetings are organized and supervised by GHANCYC:

  • Annual General Council Meeting, (highest decision making body), in September
  • Full Timers Meeting (A/DYC Chaplains and National Coordinators), in March
  • National Executive Committee Meeting, in June
  • Provisional Study Sessions, in February and March Programs
  • World Youth Days: observed/celebrated at Diocesan, National and International levels where the youth gather to witness to their faith and share their culture.
  • Provincial Youth Study Sessions: Youth are selected from A/DYCs for training in leadership, project/report writing, computer, book keeping, time management etc. for effective and efficient output in the Parishes.
